About us.

Creating BetterCommunities

Since its inception on 13 May 2016, the trust of the people in Nivasa Homes Pvt. Ltd. has been the foundation for our success and their unwavering faith catalyzes us to bring more. Families, who have created a future with us, will testify for the value that we have added to their lives.

Nivasa understands the value of trust and a customer-first attitude. That is why we have been the face of value in the real estate business in Bihar. We acknowledge the possibilities of development and innovation. That's why we incorporate technological innovations in our real estate projects and give people excellence and precision in everything.

Ultimately, providing an amazing experience to the people is our endeavor.

Mission and Vision

Creating superior value for customers, business associates, society and infrastructure investors is in our core values. To create integrated township and lifestyle patterns that provides more than just spaces to live and operate out.

Keeping our vision very close to our heart, we have become a face of trust of a new generation. We have become the language of new passion, of affluence, of happiness that begins with an individual, and culminates into the wellbeing of an entire society.

We will continue to be a brand that people trust, the brand of commitment, care and faith.

The Founder

Mr. Shashi Ranjan is the founder & director of Nivasa Homes Pvt. Ltd. A leader in quality constructions and real estate development. He has more than 15 years of work experience in the real estate industry and a brain child behind the company's outstanding reputation for quality and innovation. His projects display and ability to transform barren land into some of Bihar's most vibrant property. He build the things way ahead than just property, he builds community.
